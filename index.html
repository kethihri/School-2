Welcome!

Overview of Plymouth Creek Elementary

At Plymouth creek we practice Panther Pride. Students learn what it means to be respectful, responsible, and ready to learn. Staff and students work together to practice empathy, kindness, and our commitment to academic excellence permeates within the walls of each classroom. We know that when students feel a sense of belonging, engaged in solid peer and adult relationships and they receive appropriate academic challenges, they experience success in its fullest capacity. We value the partnership between schools, families, and strive to build those connections everyday to maximize learning for each and every student. If there's anything I can help you with or any questions I can help answer, please don't hesitate to reach out.




School Activities

Chess

Description of activity: Competitive chess for all levels of players. Wayzata students in Grades 9-12 are welcome and no experience is necessary. Where to go for more information: Activities Office. Length of season: October through March. Competitions: Minnesota State Scholastic Championship in March 2022. Eligibility requirements: Meet the Wayzata Public Schools Extra-Curricular Eligibility Requirements Grades 9-12. Student leadership: Captains and assistant coaches are selected.

Engineering Club

Calling new and returning students! The Engineering for Kids Engineering Club introduces students to a new, hands-on lesson every week from various engineering disciplines. For each lesson, students focus developing 21st Century skills such as problem solving, critical thinking, and peer collaboration. These life skills provide students with the confidence to approach challenges in their lives and realize that they can succeed in almost anything! Engineering for Kids has a library of 300+ individual lessons which continues to grow so we never have to repeat lessons. Sign up for one or all sessions!
